## 🔄 The Pipeline

How information flows through the system:

1. [[Research Agent]] discovers → logs findings in the [[Research Hub]]
2. [[Documentation Agent]] verifies → writes it into the [[Knowledge Hub]]
3. [[Monitoring Agent]] watches live conditions on [[Solana]]
4. [[Risk Agent]] scores every opportunity against [[Tokens#Token Safety Checklist|the safety checklist]]
5. [[Strategy Agent]] turns knowledge + risk scores into plans in the [[Strategy Hub]]
6. [[Execution Agent]] executes **only approved** plans via [[Wallet]] and [[Trading]]
7. [[Learning Agent]] reviews results → updates the [[Memory Hub]]

> [!warning] Safety chain
> No agent skips a step. Nothing reaches the [[Execution Agent]] without passing the [[Risk Agent]] first.
